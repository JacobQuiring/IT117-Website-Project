Jake's Diner has been serving this town since before you were born, probably. In other words, we've been making the finest dining foods all the way back in 1973. Many of our regular customers have fond memories of eating at our tables as excited kids. The same goes for the current owner. As the son of the late founder, He shows the same enthusiasm our community has always known and loved.
