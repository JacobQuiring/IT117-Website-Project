As the son of the Founder of Jake's Breakfast, Lunch, & Diner, Jack follows his father's tradition of becoming the Head Chef alongside taking ownership while apprenticing his own sons, thus passing on the skills to another generation. He has proudly studied under his father ever since he could see over the countertop.
