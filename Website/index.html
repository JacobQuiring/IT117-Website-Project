Jake's Breakfast, Lunch, & Diner is dedicated to serving traditional American dishes of all shapes and sizes, for all meals of the day, to provide you with a variety of experiences through our old and true menu and by our customer's requests. Alongside our variety of delightful food options, enjoy our many scheduled weekend events, including live music, karaoke, and comedy nights, both open mic and local artists. See old friends and make new ones when you stop by this home away from home.

See our Customer Request Page to submit your favorite diner dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner to be considered for our monthly specials. Contact us with questions about our monthly specials, weekly events, or to reserve a table for a family gathering or birthday party. Get to know our About Us Page and our History Page and learn about our longstanding role in this community.
